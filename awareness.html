Why Awareness Matters

Awareness is the first step in creating change. By understanding the issues surrounding sexual violence, harassment, and abuse, we empower individuals and communities to take action, support victims, and prevent future harm. Education and open conversations help break the silence and stigma, fostering a culture of respect and safety for all.
